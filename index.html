Welcome to my website.

My name is Saloni Sharma and I am a development professional living in New York City. I am from Zambia where I have worked previously as a consultant with a rural education non-profit.

I am currently working on my own project to launch a clean-burning gas cookstoves initiative within the capital city (Lusaka), a multi-stakeholder project that involves various ministries, NGOs, private businesses and local residents. This will be the first project of its kind in Zambia and will tackle head-on the country's top environmental problem of deforestation.
